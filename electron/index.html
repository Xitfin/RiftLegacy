WHAT IS RIFT LEGACY?

It's a simple piece of software that transforms your current League of Legends to let you rediscover the game the old way (the “Classic” style), with full respect for the visuals of that era.

We retrieved the real files from the game's old clients and the best of the modding community's work. The tool takes care of absolutely everything automatically: no need to hunt for mods or replace files manually. The app updates itself to add content, fix bugs, and remain stable.

WHAT THE APP DOES
◆
Classic mode. It automatically replaces current assets to restore the League of Legends experience of the era: classic loading screen, old skins, and more to come.
◆
Player & game info. As soon as you start a game, the app detects it automatically and displays player names, champions, ranks, and statistics for allies and opponents.
◆
Customizable options. Use Settings to enable or disable the content you want and configure the app to your preferences.
HOW TO USE IT?
1Click the “Load” button.
2Wait for the loading bar to fill up.
3Once it's ready, launch your games.
